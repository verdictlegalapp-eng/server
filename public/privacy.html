Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

Verdict is committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information across our mobile application and legal services platform.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a secure and verified environment for legal access, we collect:

  • Verification Data: Full name, professional bar license numbers, and state of practice for attorney accounts.
  • Contact Information: Mobile phone numbers (exclusively for secure OTP authentication) and email addresses.
  • Visual Assets: Profile photographs provided via your device's camera or photo library to personalize your profile and assist in identity verification.
  • Communication Logs: Records of chat interactions between clients and attorneys to ensure service quality and continuity.

2. Purpose of Collection

Your data allows us to:

  • Validate the credentials of legal professionals on our platform.
  • Ensure secure login via one-time passwords (OTP).
  • Facilitate real-time messaging and case consultations.
  • Send critical push notifications regarding your account activity.

3. Account Deletion & Data Rights

In full compliance with international privacy standards and App Store guidelines, Verdict provides users with absolute control over their data.

Immediate Data Deletion

You have the right to permanently delete your account and all associated personal data at any time. This includes your email, phone number, profile image, and communication history.

How to delete: Navigate to Profile Settings within the Verdict App and select "Permanently Delete My Account". Once confirmed, your data is wiped from our active production systems immediately.

4. Information Security

We utilize advanced encryption and secure server architectures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We do not sell or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

For any inquiries regarding your data or this policy, please reach out to our legal support team at support@verdict.sbs.
